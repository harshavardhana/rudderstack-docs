# Home

[**RudderStack**](https://rudderstack.com/) is a **customer data platform for developers.** It helps to deploy data pipelines which can collect customer data from every app, website and SaaS platform, apply custom transformations over it and further send it to various warehouses and business tools.

You can build a warehouse-first CDP that delivers complete, unified data to every part of your customer data stack by <a href="https://app.rudderstack.com/signup">signing up for RudderStack</a>.

<img src="../assets/general/basic-functionality-rudderstack.png" alt="RudderStack basic functionality diagram" />

## Key features
  - [**Event Streams**](https://rudderstack.com/docs/stream-sources/): Collect event data from all your sites and applications and route it to a wide array of customer tools and data warehouses.
  - [**Cloud Extract**](https://rudderstack.com/docs/cloud-extract-sources/): Build efficient ETL pipelines to collect event data from different cloud tools and send it to your data warehouse.
  - [**Warehouse Actions**](https://rudderstack.com/docs/warehouse-actions/): Collect data residing in your warehouse and route the enriched information to desired destinations.
